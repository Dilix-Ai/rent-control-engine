# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of background,
experience level, or technical opinion.

## Standards

Examples of expected behavior:
- Use welcoming, inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints
- Focus criticism on the work, not the person
- Help newcomers get up to speed
- Acknowledge mistakes openly

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language
- Trolling or sustained disruption
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Conduct that would be unacceptable in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying these standards and
will take appropriate corrective action. Maintainers may remove or
reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Report violations to **ops@dilix.ai**. All reports will be reviewed
confidentially.
